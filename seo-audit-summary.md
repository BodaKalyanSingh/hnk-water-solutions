## Remaining Recommendations

These items require external tools, assets, or ongoing effort beyond the website build:

1. **Replace Google verification code** — Update `GOOGLE_SITE_VERIFICATION` in `src/lib/constants.ts` and rename `googleyour-google-verification-code.html` with your actual code from Google Search Console.

2. **Add real images** — Upload logo (`/images/logo.png`), OG image (`/images/og-image.jpg`), service photos, and team photos. Add `srcset`/`sizes` for responsive serving.

3. **Deploy with production cache headers** — Configure your hosting (Netlify, Vercel, Cloudflare, or nginx) to set `Cache-Control: public, max-age=31536000, immutable` for static assets.

4. **Submit sitemap to Google Search Console** — After deployment, submit `https://hanamkondawaterservice.com/sitemap.xml`.

5. **Google Business Profile** — Follow `google-business-profile-checklist.md` to optimize your GBP listing.

6. **Collect Google reviews** — Target 5+ reviews per month. Current schema shows 4.9/87 — update `buildReviewSchema()` when real numbers are available.

7. **Local link building** — List business on JustDial, Sulekha, IndiaMART, and Telangana local directories with consistent NAP.

8. **Monthly blog posts** — Publish 1–2 articles per month (e.g. "Best RO Purifier for Borewell Water in Hanamkonda") to build topical authority.

9. **Google Ads** — Run local search ads targeting "water purifier repair hanamkonda" and similar high-intent keywords.

10. **Core Web Vitals monitoring** — After launch, monitor LCP, INP, and CLS in Google Search Console and PageSpeed Insights. Add a real hero image with preload once photography is ready.

11. **SSL certificate** — Ensure HTTPS is active on `hanamkondawaterservice.com` before going live.

12. **Analytics** — Connect Google Analytics 4 and track WhatsApp clicks, phone calls, and contact form submissions as conversion events.
